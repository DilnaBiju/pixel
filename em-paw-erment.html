Rules and Regulations:
  • All pets must be accompanied by their owners at all times.
  • Participants must arrive at least 30 minutes before their performance slot.

  • No aggressive behavior or disruptive actions will be tolerated.
  • Each performance should not exceed 3 minutes.
  • Bring all necessary props and equipment required for your pet's act.
